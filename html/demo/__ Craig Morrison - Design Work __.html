Rabble.org is a startup designed to take advantage of crowdfunding advertising budgets for social and political messages, in order to further the awareness of these causes.

The challenge was attempting to communicate through proper design and copy that donating money meant more people would see the message you felt so passionaltely about.

Services: Creative Direction, UX Design, Interface Design, Animation

Minoe is a local productivity network designed specifically for Mothers. It is meant to connect Moms to other people, places and events in their neighbhourhood.

Services: UX Design, Interface Design, Branding, Marketing

Brunch Toronto was created after growing frustrated with the complexity and level of commitment required when using location service apps, such as Yelp or TripAdvisor, to find niche restaurants. I wanted to create a simplified map that displayed all the most popular brunch locations in the city. It uses Javascript and the Google Maps API to display the locations and is updated with a customized backend CMS.

Services: Art direction, Branding, Front End Development

TradeRater.com is a startup I founded and designed which aims to connect local contractors to prospective clients in an oDesk.com format.

Clients post job offers for work they need completed. Contractors then receive alerts when a new job is posted matching their line of work.

Services: Concept, UX Design, Interface Design, Branding

Vagabond Digital was looking for a clean brand that communicated all the different services they offer. The idea was to create an identity that didn't focus on one specific service and stay from tech related imagery. Their logo is simply their name, only the letters are slightly askew in places, to communicate the idea behind their brand, which is that they differ slightly from other businesses in the same field.

Services: Art Direction, Branding, Front End Development, Responsive Design
